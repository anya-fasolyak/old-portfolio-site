    Futurist Manifesto

    This book uses industrial processes to transcribe the text of the Futurist Manifesto by Filippo Marinetti onto industrial materials; the text is laser-engraved onto plexiglass and anodized aluminum. The book utilizes the flag structure to show the 11 points of the manifesto.
